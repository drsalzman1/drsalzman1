Sudoku Tutor

App Purpose: Learn to solve Sudoku puzzles using the Nishio (backtrack) method.

Game Objective: Enter the digits 1 through 9 once in each of the puzzle's nine rows, nine columns, and nine 3x3 boxes.

Standard Automation:
  • Mark blanks with unused digits
  • Shade blanks with no marks red
  • Shade single marks green
  • Shade double marks yellow
  • Prevent entry of used digits
  • Select the blank with the fewest marks

Optional Automation:

Puzzle Entry:
Enter Puzzle: Enter each of the pre-filled squares from the puzzle you want to solve. To copy a square: select (touch) a destination square, then enter (touch) its source number on the keypad. To erase the most recent square: touch "Back".
Solve Puzzle: Enter the value of each blank. To enter a value: select the blank with the fewest marks, then enter one of its marks. If you hit a dead end (red): touch "Back" until you return to a blank with an untried mark, then enter one of its untried marks.
